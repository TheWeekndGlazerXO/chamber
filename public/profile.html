Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. Billing is prorated and changes take effect immediately.
What counts as a "report"? +
Running any feature (Full Analysis, Ad Copy, SEO Keywords, etc.) uses one report credit. The Margin Calculator is always free and doesn't count.
Is the Reddit and news data actually live? +
Yes. Every analysis fetches real Reddit posts, YouTube videos, and news articles at run time via live APIs — not cached or simulated data.
Do you offer refunds? +
If you're not satisfied within the first 7 days, email us for a full refund — no questions asked.
What AI model does Chamber use? +
All analysis uses GPT-4o on every plan. We don't downgrade the model on lower tiers.
Do annual plans renew automatically? +
Yes, annual plans auto-renew at the same rate unless cancelled. You'll get a reminder email 7 days before renewal.
