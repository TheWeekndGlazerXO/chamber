01
Getting started
You're in. Here's the 3-minute setup.
🏠
Your dashboard is your home base
The dashboard shows your recent analyses, quick-start feature cards, and usage tracker at the top. The sidebar on the left (click ◫) reveals all 50+ features organized by category.
The ⌕ Search bar (or ⌘K) lets you jump to any feature in under 2 seconds.
⚡
Run your first Full Analysis
Click the ⚡ icon in the left rail, type a product name (e.g. "Wireless Earbuds Pro"), and hit Generate. Takes 20–60 seconds. Chamber runs 6 intelligence modules simultaneously — Reddit, web search, competitor research, AI scoring, influencer matching, and action planning.
Add a product URL and target audience for significantly more precise results.
📊
Read your Growth Score
Your Growth Score (0–100) is the headline number — it combines market timing, search demand, competition gap, audience fit, and trend momentum. 80+ is a strong signal. 60–80 needs validation. Under 60 requires a pivot.
Click "Open Full Report →" in the output header to see the full report.html view with PDF export.
📋
Access history and past reports
Click "History" in the top bar to pull up your analysis drawer. All past analyses are saved automatically and accessible from the dashboard home screen. Click any report to reload it in the output pane.
02
Understanding the Full Analysis
What each module does and how to act on it.
📊 Growth Score
Composite 0–100 based on 5 weighted factors. Use it as a quick go/no-go signal. Don't launch anything under 55 without a differentiated angle.
🎯 Marketing Angles
4–6 positioning angles ranked by strength (1–5). The top angle should become your hero headline. Test 2–3 angles in your first ad split test.
🔍 Competitors
Each competitor shows a threat level (0–10) and a Gap — the specific weakness you can exploit. The Gap column is where you find your winning angle.
📊 Reddit Sentiment
Real community opinions pulled from relevant subreddits. The Key Themes are the exact phrases your customers use — paste them directly into ad copy for higher CTR.
🤳 Influencer Matches
Micro-influencers sorted by match score. Send DMs to the top 3 — micro-influencers (under 100k) typically have 3–5x better engagement and accept commission deals.
💡 Action Plan
7 prioritized next steps. Do them in order. They're sequenced from cheapest/fastest to highest leverage, not alphabetically.
📄 Full Report View
After any analysis, click "Open Full Report →" in the output header. This opens report.html with a full-page view including keywords, market sizing, and PDF export. Store it in sessionStorage so you can share the URL or download the PDF.

05
Pro tips
How to get 10x more value from Chamber.
🎯
Add audience context for better results
Instead of just "Wireless Earbuds", try "Wireless Earbuds Pro — Target audience: Remote workers 25-40 who take 5+ calls/day". The AI tailors every module — marketing angles, influencer selection, Reddit sentiment — to your specific buyer.
🔗
Chain features together
Run Full Analysis → copy the marketing angle title → paste it into Ad Copy → take the top ad headline → run it through Viral Hooks for TikTok. The output of one feature feeds the next. This is how you build a full launch campaign in 20 minutes.
The margin calculator is separate from AI limits — use it as many times as you want.
📤
Export and share reports
After any analysis, click "Open Full Report →" to get the full-page view. From there, hit "Export PDF" for a branded PDF to share with co-founders, investors, or clients. The "Copy JSON" and "Copy MD" buttons in the output pane let you pipe data into other tools.
🔴
Use Reddit sentiment as ad copy source
The Key Themes from Reddit sentiment are exact phrases your customers use organically. These perform 2–3x better in ad headlines than agency-written copy because they're already in your customer's vocabulary.
Sort sample posts by upvotes — high-upvote posts signal which pain points resonate most.
📊
Run analyses monthly, not once
Markets shift. Run a new full analysis every 30 days to catch trend velocity changes, new competitors, and seasonal shifts before they hurt you. The History drawer makes it easy to compare across time.
💰
Always run the Margin Calculator first
Before you run any analysis on a product, calculate your true unit economics. If your gross profit is negative after fees, ads, and returns, no amount of marketing intelligence will save the business. Fix the economics first.
Include platform fees (15% Amazon/Etsy), ad spend (15–25% of revenue), and returns (5–12% depending on category).
